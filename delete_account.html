Request Account Deletion

This page allows you to request the deletion of your account and associated data from our apps, "Sudoku and Puzzle King," developed by B. Kostov.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email our support team at gamingwithbujo@gmail.com.
  2. Include your account details: Username, Email, and current Coins balance.
  3. We will confirm your request within 7 days and send an email once your data has been deleted.

Data Deletion Details

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account details (username, email, etc.)
  • Game progress

A confirmation email will be sent to you after the data deletion process is completed. Please note that certain data may be retained temporarily for legal and security reasons.
